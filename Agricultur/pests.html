Pest Control & Crop Protection

Identify common pests, prevention methods and safe control techniques

üêû Common Agricultural Pests

Aphids

Small insects that suck plant sap and crops.

  • Affected Crops: Vegetables, fruits
  • Damage: Leaf curling, yellowing
  • Control: Neem oil, soap spray
Stem Borer

Larvae bore into stems causing plant death.

  • Affected Crops: Rice, maize
  • Damage: Dead hearts
  • Control: Pheromone traps
Whitefly

Transmits viral diseases and reduces crop yield.

  • Affected Crops: Cotton, tomato
  • Damage: Leaf discoloration
  • Control: Yellow sticky traps

üõ° Pest Control Methods

Cultural Control

Crop rotation, clean cultivation and resistant varieties.

Biological Control

Use of natural enemies like ladybirds and parasites.

Mechanical Control

Hand picking, traps and barriers.

Chemical Control

Controlled use of pesticides as last option.
